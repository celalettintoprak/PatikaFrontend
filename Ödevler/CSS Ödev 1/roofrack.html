Aerodynamic and quiet

Turtle roof racks are low-profile, aerodynamic roof rack system for an exceptionally quiet ride. Its state-of-the-art low profile design perfectly complements the car’s roof-line. The advanced aerodynamic shape minimizes noise and improves fuel efficiency.

Turtle roof racks safely carry loads up to 75 kg and has integrated T-tracks for easy installation of sports and cargo carriers.
