As we,

CAN AUTO, whose foundations were established in 1979, has become one of the largest and most successful companies in Turkey's automotive sector, always prioritizing quality, aesthetics and ergonomics in its production journey of more than 40 years.

By combining advanced and world-class machine line with it's experience, it has been one of the rare companies that delivers high quality, low cost to consumers.

It aims to become a leader company in its industry with unique designs, high quality products and the idea that it always takes on customer satisfaction as a result of its investments in Innovation, R&D and P&D.
